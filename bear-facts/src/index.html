Bear facts

February, 2019

This two player card game is made with ES6 modules. It's a great way to learn about bears! Take turns choosing and reading out a category (e.g. height, value 1.8 metres). The highest value wins, and the winner keeps both cards, which move to the bottom of the winner's stack. In the case of a draw, both players keep their card. The player who gains all the cards wins the game.
