Brexit: how we voted

June, 2016

This map (technically known as a choropleth) shows how people of the United Kingdom and Gibraltar voted in the EU referendum. There were clear regional differences, with Scotland, London and parts of Northern Ireland (and me) keen to remain part of the EU, and the other regions wanting a 'brexit'. Perhaps more striking though, is how close it was. The final result: 51.9% voted to leave; 48.1% voted to remain.

Data: Electoral Commission
